Welcome

Welcome to datapraz. This is my attempt to contribute some of my own insights from the treasure trove of open data being generated at a frenetic pace around the world over the past few years. Although i do not have partiality for a certain topic , you will mostly find socially useful commentary in these pages. It is of utmost importance for me to ensure that what i present here is completely accurate and i will include as much source information for all building blocks as possible. Hope you enjoy the stay here and add some valuable commentary of yours as well if you find the insight new or useful.
